Bhel Puri
01

Ingredients Name

  • * Puffed rice ,
  • Roasted peanut
  • * Salted rice(Namkeen)
  • * Chopped tomato ,
  • Chopped onion
  • * Chopped coriander
  • * Chopped pepper
  • * Salt (according to taste)
  • * Chat masala
  • * juice of lemon (0.5ml to 1ml)

02

Procedure to Follows:-

  1. first mix the salt,roasted peanut, chopped (tomato,onion,coriander,pepper), salt(according to taste),chat masala,juice of lemon in the bowl
  2. then,mix the puffed rice and salted rice with above ingredients
  3. after then proper mix it
  4. now ready the bhel puri taste or serve :-)
